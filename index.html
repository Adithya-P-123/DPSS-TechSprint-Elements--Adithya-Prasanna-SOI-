The Red Rocks Amphitheatre- Colorado, the United States

Background

The Red Rocks Amphitheatre, also known simply as the "Red Rocks", is a natural, one of it's kind amphitheatre. It is the world's only natural amphitheatre and on top of it, is also acoustically perfectly sounding. The construction of the added elements of the amphiheatre began in 1936, after the Red Rocks was purchased in 1927 by the City of Denver.
There have been many famous artists who have performed in the Red Rocks, including the Beatles and Jimi Hendrix.
